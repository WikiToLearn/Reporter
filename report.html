Two weeks of WikiToLearn...

06 September 2016 - 21 September 2016

Like all good things, QtCon has come to an end. It has been an amazing summit. We shared our ideas, met new people interested in our project and worked on lot of new features for our site. Lot of people want us to go to their countries to talk about WikiToLearn, are willing to participate and contribute with us to the diffusion of free knowledge all around the world. Our developers are coding, coding and coding for you. We are preparing a huge event in Amrita University (India) to introduce people to WikiToLearn and to create new local communities.

In a nutshell:

Code: 573 changes, 1321 lines inserted, 4332 lines removed.
Content: 12 edits on 53 pages.
